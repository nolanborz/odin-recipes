Voiceless Voice Recipe

The Voiceless Voice Core

These are the staples within the Madolche archetype you'll be using to build your deck list. While your ratios may fluctuate, these cards are the bare-minumum you'll want to have on hand.

Main Deck Monsters

  • 3x Lo, the Prayers of the Voiceless Voice
  • 3x Saffira, Dragon Queen of the Voiceless Voice
  • 1x Sauravis, Dragon Sage of the Voiceless Voice
  • 2x Skull Guardian, Protector of the Voiceless Voice
  • 2x Sauravis, the Ancient and Ascended

Main Deck Spells

  • 3x Barrier of the Voiceless Voice
  • 2x Prayers of the Voiceless Voice

Main Deck Traps

  • 1x Radiance of the Voiceless Voice

The Voiceless Gameplay Loop

Here is a basic combo you can use to get your plays started.

  1. Discard Saffira, Dragon Queen of the Voiceless Voice to send 1 Prayers of the Voiceless Voice to the GY and add 1 Skull Guardian,
    Protector of the Voiceless Voice to your hand.
  2. Activate Lo's effect to add Barrier of the Voiceless Voice to your hand.
  3. Banish Saffira from your GY to Ritual Summon Skull Guardian from your by tributing Lo (from your hand).
  4. Activate Lo as chain link 1 and Skull Guardian as chain link 2.
  5. Resolving the previous chain, add 1 Sauravis, the Ancient and Ascended from deck to hand, and SS Lo from the GY in defense position.
  6. Activate Lo's effect and activate 1 Barrier of the Voiceless Voice from your deck.

This is a basic gameplay loop for Voiceless Voice using some of its key cards. Your lines should differ depending on on your hand.
